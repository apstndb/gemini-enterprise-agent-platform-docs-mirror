This document describes how to use the AI assistant in Vertex AI Studio to help you refine and generate prompts. AI assistant can speed up your prompt writing process.

## Refine prompts

The AI assistant in Vertex AI Studio can help you refine your prompts based on your targeted results.

To refine prompts, do the following:

1.  Open Vertex AI Studio and click **Create prompt** :

2.  In the prompt box, add your prompt.

3.  Click **Submit** .

4.  Click auto\_fix **Refine prompt** .

5.  Enter feedback about what you'd like to change about the model's response to your prompt, or select one or more of the following prefilled feedback options:
    
      - Make shorter
      - Maker longer
      - More professional
      - More casual

6.  Click **Apply and run** .

7.  Repeat the process until you're satisfied with the model's response.

## Generate prompts

Use the Vertex AI Studio AI prompt assistant to generate your prompts. Generating prompts can help you speed up the prompt-writing process.

To generate prompts, do the following:

1.  Open Vertex AI Studio and click **Create prompt** :

2.  In the prompt box, click **Help me write** .

3.  Enter a description of what you would like your prompt to achieve. For example, `Music recommendation bot` .

4.  Click **Generate prompt** .

5.  Click **Insert** .

6.  Click **Submit** .

7.  If you aren't satisfied with the model's response, delete the prompt then repeat the process.
